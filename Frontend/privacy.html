Privacy Policy

Welcome to Samnwadh. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

When you use our platform, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details when you sign up.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our platform, such as pages visited and actions taken.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services.

  • Send updates, newsletters, or support communications (if opted in).

4. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our platform uses end-to-end encryption for secure messaging.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except when required by law or to enhance platform functionality (e.g., analytics tools).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Request a copy of your stored information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@samnwadh.com
